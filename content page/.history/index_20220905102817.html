Aparelhos que transformam energia elétrica em calor, como geladeiras e chuveiros, são os que mais impactam na conta de luz. No entanto, sem uso consciente, até mesmo itens como computadores e videogames podem pesar no final do mês. In 2021, there was a 20% increase in the account electricity, and an additional fee of R$ 14 started to be charged every 100 kWh (kilowatt-hour, measure of energy) consumed. For save on expenses, understand how the devices you uses work can make a difference in the price of the bill at the end of the month. Appliances that convert electrical energy into heat, such as refrigerators and showers, are the ones that most impact the electricity bill. Nonetheless, without conscious use, even items such as computers and video games can weigh in at the end of the month.
